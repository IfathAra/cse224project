Cubers Choice was first started on August 2019 by an agonized Cuber who wanted a cubing related shop which could give him the necessary specs that he needed at a understandable price. So he became the change what he wished to see in the world. Although It wasn’t given a name at that time. At first it started from a Capital of Zero Bangladeshi Taka by taking pre-orders from the customers. Soon it started growing due to the customer service and real time update. In time, Cubenation became very popular and now we can proudly say that we are one of the Top Cubeshops in Bangladesh(Not the best because we are not the judge here).
